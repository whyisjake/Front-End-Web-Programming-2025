About Me

I'm an innovative technology leader with 15+ years of experience transforming complex systems into scalable, high-impact solutions. Currently serving as Senior Director of Customer Success at Automattic, I lead teams of 50+ professionals while spearheading major initiatives and global account expansion. As Security Release Lead and core committer for WordPress, I direct critical security processes for one of the world's most popular content management systems.

My journey spans from teaching graphic design and web development at Stevens-Henager College to engineering high-traffic digital properties at WIRED and O'Reilly Media. I've architected integrations featured at Apple WWDC, created plugins showcased at Facebook F8, and authored "Bootstrap: Responsive Web Development" for O'Reilly Media. With a Master's in Integrated Design, Business, and Technology from USC, I now share my passion for web technologies as Adjunct Faculty, mentoring the next generation of developers.

When I'm not leading enterprise transformations or contributing to open source projects, you can find me coaching youth baseball and softball, exploring emerging web technologies, or building tools that make the web more accessible for everyone.
